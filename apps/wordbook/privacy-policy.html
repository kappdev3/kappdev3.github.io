What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application uses Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google, to collect anonymous or pseudonymized data about your in-app activity. This data helps us understand how users interact with the app and improve the overall user experience.

The information collected through Firebase Analytics may include:

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or location through Firebase Analytics.

The data collected through Firebase Analytics is used for the following purposes:
